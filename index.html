The Studio

Neeraj Films is built on the belief that the most powerful special effect is a truthful performance.

From first draft to final mix, we stay close to the story—protecting its heart while expanding its reach.

Based in United States of America and collaborating globally, Neeraj Films develops and produces narrative features, limited series, and select branded stories. We partner with writers and directors who bring a strong point of view, and we surround them with crews that honour both craft and care.

Our slate focuses on character driven stories with emotional stakes, often set against legal, social, or intimate family backdrops. We aim for work that can travel to festivals, streamers, and hearts with equal ease.
